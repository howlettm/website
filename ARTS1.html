This homepage was created by Rebecca Raglon for use in her sections of the Art1 program at the University of British Columbia. The Arts1 program is an innovative first year program for students in the Faculty of Arts at UBC. Arts1 courses are taught by an interdisciplinary team of faculty from a number of Departments located throughout the University. In 1995-1996 the theme for this section of the Arts1 Program is "Nature vs Nurture".
